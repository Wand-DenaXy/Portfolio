The Story Behind The Code

I'm a passionate backend and full stack developer who finds beauty in the logic that powers great software. From designing resilient APIs to managing complex database architectures, I love making systems that actually work at scale.

My journey started with curiosity — breaking things to understand how they worked. That curiosity evolved into a drive for clean architecture, system design, and building real-world products that solve actual problems.

I'm particularly drawn to backend engineering, automation, and the intersection of software with business logic. Whether it's building payment systems, managing multi-tenant SaaS platforms, or designing data pipelines — I turn complex requirements into elegant solutions.
